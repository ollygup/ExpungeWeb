FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is Expunge really free to use?

    Yes. There are no paid tiers, no feature paywalls, and no account required. The complete redaction suite — text search, draw-to-redact, and OCR-based redaction — is available at no cost.

  • Do I need to create an account?

    No. Expunge requires no account, no email, and no login of any kind. Open the app and start redacting immediately.

  • Are free online PDF redaction tools safe to use?

    Many free tools upload your files to a remote server, which is a significant privacy risk. Expunge processes everything locally in your browser — files are never transmitted, stored externally, or accessed by any third party.

  • Does free redaction actually remove text permanently?

    Expunge uses MuPDF compiled to WebAssembly to perform true content-stream redaction. Unlike tools that draw a black box over text, Expunge removes the underlying characters from the PDF data — recovery is not possible.

  • Is there a file size or page count limit?

    No. Because processing happens in your browser, limits depend on your device's memory rather than a server-imposed cap. Most modern computers handle hundreds of pages without issue.
